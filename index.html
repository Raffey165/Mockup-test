WHAT WE DO IN CODING.BN

Coding.bn is a 6-month course program initaied by AITI Brunei to train unemployed youths with coding/programming skills aligned with demand in the industry. During these 6 months course and mentorship program, participants will have the opportunity to undertake apprenticeship in companies to work on real projects allowing them to experience a real working environment! In the Coding Academy, we will teach you more than just coding skils. You will learn how to solve problems and be a self-sufficent developer
